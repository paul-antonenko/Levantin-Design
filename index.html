About Us

Having started in 2012 with epathetic experiments in furniture restoration, this experience soon helped us to form a studio of object design, and to produce our first industrial samples.
Levantin design is a modern brand of author 's furniture founded by brothers Sergei and Aleksandr Lvov. It has gathered around itself a team of enthusiasts and independent craftsmen.
Our approach to design is characterised by the concept of Wow in your space: "realising bold and cosmopolitan ideas, demanded by people with a creative outlook on the world!".
